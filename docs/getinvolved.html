Get Involved in ManyDogs

There are multiple ways that you can get involved in ManyDogs, and contributors at all stages of their careers (Undergraduate to Professor Emeritus) are very welcome to join the project.

The Overall Project

The ManyDogs Project is a large group of researchers who hope to conduct a number of studies over the next several years. If you’re interested in becoming a member of the ManyDogs project and helping out with behavioral data collection, administration, and/or creative roles, please complete the Member Registration Form. If you’re not able to help with the research we’re conducting but are interested in what we’re working on, check out our publications.

ManyDogs Projects

ManyDogs 1 has completed, and the manuscript with the main experimental results is in press at Animal Behavior and Cognition. But we are soliciting ideas for the next project. If you have a great project idea, check out the call for project ideas.

Other Big Team Science Groups

We’re thrilled to partner with other big team science, multi-lab consortiums that promote open science practices and collaboration. If you’re not able to get involved in ManyDogs, feel free to check out other big team science groups.
